Persian Cat
HISTORY

The first documented ancestors of the Persian
were imported from Khorasan, Persia, into
Italy in 1620 by Pietro della Valle, and from
Angora (now Ankara) Ottoman Empire (Turkey),
into France by Nicholas-Claude Fabri de
Peiresc at around the same time. The Khorasan
cats were grey coated while those from Angora
were white. From France, they soon reached Britain.

APPEARANCE
